About AsSip

AsSip is a tribute to the land where tea is not just grown, but lived. Born in Assam, the home of the world’s finest tea, AsSip was created with a simple purpose — to share the true essence of Assam with everyone, without compromise. We believe tea should be pure, honest and untouched by anything unnecessary. That’s why every batch of AsSip is crafted with care, using natural ingredients and traditional methods that preserve the richness of the leaf. No chemicals, no artificial additions — just the authentic depth and character that Assam tea is known for. Our promise is simple: To deliver tea that feels genuine, premium, and connected to its roots. Every jar we pack carries the warmth of our culture, the dedication of our growers, and the timeless spirit of Assam. With each sip, you taste not just tea, but the story of a land that has perfected it over generations. AsSip — One Sip of Assam.

From the Founder and Team AsSip

Growing up in Assam, tea was never just a drink for me — it was a feeling, a connection to our land and our people. I always wanted to serve the true taste and essence of rich Assam tea, without chemicals, without artificial flavours — only pure, honest tea the way it’s meant to be.

With AsSip, that dream has finally taken shape. Every blend we create is handpicked, crafted with care, and stays true to the natural goodness of Assam.

As a team, we are building AsSip slowly and honestly — small-batch, focused on quality, and always rooted in Assam. When you choose AsSip, you’re supporting local growers, real flavours, and a story that started in our home.

— Md Sarfraj Ali, Founder & CEO
