About AsSip

AsSip is a boutique tea brand from Assam. We harvest handpicked leaves from small gardens, process using artisanal methods, and pack with minimal intervention so the garden character stays in the cup.

  • Single-origin Assam
  • Organic & small-batch
  • Care for growers & the planet
